Self-portrait collage

As a part of Computer graphics and Visual communication courses, I created a self-portrait collage. I chose a concept of a mask. At first sight all you see is colorful flowers. But the point was to show that many times we see just the mask and not what is under it.

Infographics

The task was to conduct an interview, collect information about a groupmate, choose one outstanding or unusual fact about this groupmate and then create infographics on the topic. It was a great chance to practice my graphic and communicational skills as well.

My groupmate is fond of ballet, so my topic was "Sapir and Ballet".
